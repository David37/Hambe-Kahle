About Hamba Kahle

Hamba Kahle Transportation is a transportation company that was founded in 2019 in Grahamstown, Eastern Cape, which is where their headquarter offices are situated.
Hamba Kahle is derived from the South African language, IsiZulu which directly translates to "go well". This goes very well with our motto of "Let us drive you safely".
Hamba Kahle prides itself in their service of safely transporting individuals or groups to their selective destinations across the beautiful South Africa.
However, Hamba Kahle does not provide services of tours around that specific destination and accomodation. Our service is to safely drive you to or from your destination.
We provide transportation for families, friends, colleagues, and any other group or individuals that are travelling and need transportation.
Firstly, you would need to create an account with Hamba Kahle to have access to our lovely services. Once, you have an account then all be a click away from us transporting you.
The account will allow you as the client to make a booking with us. There will be a form that you would need to fill out with the details of your booking.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to enable safe travel in country of South Africa. To fulfill customers' need of transportation by providing quality service.


Our Mission

Our mission as Hamba Kahle Transportation is to work by the following values:
-To provide safe and dependable transportation for our passengers to ensure that the journey is safe as possible.
-Having affordable prices that benefit our shareholders while not digging deep into our clients' pockets.
-To have employees that are respectful and honest so that good service can be received by clients.
-To be committed into providing the best services that exceed client expectation.
-Finally, we aim to grow as a business with the great support from our employees and clients.


Our Vision

The vision is to continue to provide great value services to our clients by adhering to our mission.
We aim to deliver the best services while making a good return for our shareholders.

Meet Our Team
