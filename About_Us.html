About Hamba Kahle

Hamba Kahle is a transportation company that was founded in 2019 in Grahamstown, Eatern Cape, which is where their headquarter offices are situated.
Hamba Kahle prides itself in their service of safely transporting individuals or groups to their selective destinations across the beautiful South Africa.
However, Hamba Kahle does not provide services of tours around that specific destination and accomodation. Our service is to safely drive you to or from your destination.
Firstly, you would need to create an account with Hamba Kahle to have access to our lovely services. Once, you have an account then all be a click away from us transporting you.
The account will allow you as the client to make a booking with us. There will be a form that you would need to fill out with the details of your booking.
