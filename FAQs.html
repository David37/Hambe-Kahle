Frequently Asked Questions


You need to register an account on the Homepage of Hamba Kahle Transportation. Once, you have created an account then all of the services are available to you. There is a booking option that you need to click on to start your process. It will lead you to the booking form where you will have to fill in the necessary details. Please make sure that the information that you fill in is the correct details of your trip.


The amount that you will have to pay depends on the details of your trip. To explain further, the travel distance is mostly what your payment depends on. Another factor that contributes to the price is the time of year that you are travelling in. Example, if it is during the festive or Easter season where demand for transportation service is high then price is likely to increase as well. However, the total price remains affordable for our clients.

On the About Us page, there are banking details for Hamba Kahle Transportation. Please use your username as the reference when making payment so that it is known who the payment is from. Your payment will be confirmation of your booking, therefore please make payment after booking is made on your account.

The person that made the booking should have carry their identification document and proof of travel (ticket with travel details sent via email).

Passengers are allowed 30 kg of luggage. However, if the passengers have more than 30 kg of luggage then they can indicate on their booking that they require a trailer for travelling. The trailer is medium-sized and enclosed therefore can accomodate more luggage for the passengers. The additional trailer will be at no extra cost.

Yes. The number of stops will depend on the length of the journey. The stops will be at petrol stations with bathrooms and food outlets. The driver will allow the stop to be approximately 15 - 20 minutes.

Yes, pregnant persons are allowed to travel using Hamba Kahle Transportation. However, if the pregnant passenger is over 7 months pregnant then they will require a medical certificate stating that they are fit to travel.

Yes. We as Hamba Kahle Transportation do accomodate passengers with special needs. If extra space is required for a wheelchair then a trailer will be provided.

Alcohol is not allowed to be consumed on our transport while travelling. Intoxicated persons will not be allowed on our transport because of behaviour associated with intoxicated persons. Any form of recreational drugs are not to be used during travel. Smoking is also prohibited and this includes electronic smoking as well. It is important that these rules be followed.

No. Animals are not allowed on Hamba Kahle Transportation cars except for guide dogs accompanying blind people.

Yes, you can cancel a booking that you have made with us. You will have to either make a call or email us for your cancelation. If the cancelation is made 48 hours or more before the departure date then 60% will be refunded to the client. But, if a client cancels less than 48 hours before departure date then only 35% will be refunded.

Yes, you can make changes to your booking but these changes will have to be made 48 hours before the 'old' departure date. You will have to call or email us to make these booking changes. This however does not guarantee that there will be available vehicles for your new trip but everything will be communicated to you by our office. If we have a full booking for your new trip then you will receive your refund. If there is space then your new trip details will be emailed to you.
